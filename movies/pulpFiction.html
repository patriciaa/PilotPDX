Pulp Fiction

The lives of two mob hit men, a boxer, a gangster's wife, and a pair of diner bandits intertwine in four tales of violence and redemption. (imdb.com)

Role of Women (1-10)

Pulp fiction has a variety of female characters. Fabienne is a rather silly and childish character who appeard dependent on her boyfriend, Butch. On the other end of the spectrum, Yolanda fearlessly robs a diner with her partner Ringo. Somewhat in the middle of the two is Mia who over-doses on Cocane and tries to cheat on her husband. The portrayal of women in Pulp Fiction is mixed, and there are significantly fewer female chracters than male, presumably because Pulp Fiction is primarily an action movie.
